What is Microblading?

You may have heard about microblading and microshading, but perhaps you are not sure about what they are. Simply put both are forms of semi-permanent cosmetic tattooing that imitates the look of full natural eyebrows.

With microblading ,a bladed needle is used to apply pigment under the epidermis top-layers in hair-stroke patterns.By contrast, microshding uses pin-like dots using a specialized machine to create a powdered look brow look for clients looking to achieve more depth to their existing brows.
